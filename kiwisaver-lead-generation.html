KiwiSaver: The Numbers You Need to Know

According to the Inland Revenue, over 3 million New Zealanders are enrolled in KiwiSaver as of 2023. Yet, a considerable percentage remain in default funds or have minimal engagement with their account. Check out some key stats:

Statistic Figure (Approx.)
Total KiwiSaver Members 3.1 million
Default Fund Holders ~420,000
Avg. Contribution Rate 3-4%
Active vs. Passive Fund Ratio ~60% Active / 40% Passive

*Data compiled from IRD & Commission for Financial Capability estimates.

Effective Tactics for KiwiSaver Lead Generation

  • Educate About Funds: Many KiwiSaver members don’t realise they can switch from default or underperforming funds—highlight the benefits of better fund selection.
  • Demystify Fees & Returns: Offer quick assessments or mini-comparisons, showing how slight fee reductions can yield thousands in retirement savings.
  • Host Online Webinars: Brief, free KiwiSaver sessions attract people seeking clarity, leading to direct inquiries post-webinar.
  • Leverage Milestones: Target those turning 18 or starting a first job—key moments when KiwiSaver decisions matter most.
  • Pay-Per-Show Appointments: Services like Leadspresso fill your calendar with real prospects. Less chasing, more advising.
