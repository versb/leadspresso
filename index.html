Quality Lead Checklist

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you generate leads?

We use multiple outbound channels—like a skilled barista mixing different ingredients—to reach potential clients. Only those genuinely interested in financial advice end up at your table.

What if I receive low-quality leads?

We work hard to filter them out, and if any low-quality leads slip through, you won’t pay for them. It’s as simple as that.

Do you cover all types of insurance?

We focus on personal insurance lines—life, health, income, trauma, mortgage—and KiwiSaver. We don’t handle general insurance like car or contents.

How soon can I expect leads?

Typically within a few days of your onboarding. We fire up the outbound “espresso machine” quickly so you can start receiving prospects.

Is there a long-term commitment?

No. Our service is flexible—pause, scale up, or slow down as your business needs evolve.

How do I handle payments?

We bill periodically based on delivered leads or attended meetings—whichever plan you choose. Payment cycles are discussed during onboarding.

What about compliance?

We’re a lead generation service, not financial advisers. We follow NZ’s Privacy Act 2020 when handling data. Actual advice is provided by the registered professionals we connect you with.
