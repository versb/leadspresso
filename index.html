Our Appointment Standards

  • Relevant Need: Mortgage, children, or existing coverage interest.
  • Age Range: 25–65 years old.
  • Health Status: No critical pre-existing conditions that prohibit coverage.
  • Intent: Willing and ready to discuss personal insurance or KiwiSaver advice.
  • Income Level: Generally above $50k per year.
  • No Existing Adviser: Not currently locked in with another broker.
  • Reliability: Demonstrated commitment to attending scheduled appointments.

We also have a separate checklist for KiwiSaver-only appointments—just ask if that’s your main focus.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you generate these appointments?

We rely on personal outreach—calls, emails, texts—to confirm real interest before scheduling them on your calendar.

What if someone doesn’t show up?

You pay nothing. Our model charges you solely for appointments that actually take place.

Do you integrate with my CRM?

Yes, we can sync with your CRM for attendance verification, lead tracking, and data updates. We also use Calendly to streamline booking.

What about KiwiSaver prospects?

We specialize in KiwiSaver too. The process is similar—only pay if they show—and we tailor our approach to match KiwiSaver-specific needs.

How quickly can I start receiving appointments?

Often within days of completing our onboarding process. Once we have your schedule and preferences, we launch our outreach immediately.

What does onboarding look like?

You’ll fill out a brief setup form—taking about 30 minutes of your time—then we handle everything from outreach to scheduling.

What if I’m not satisfied with the lead quality or ROI?

We’ll review your feedback, analyze our approach, and refine our efforts. Data is key, and we use it to continuously improve.

Which types of insurance do you handle?

Primarily personal lines: life, health, income protection, trauma, and mortgage protection. We don’t handle general lines like car or contents.

Is there a long-term commitment?

No. You can pause or stop any time. We customize everything to match your practice’s current needs.

How do I pay?

We invoice for attended appointments weekly or monthly. For non-standard leads, you pay a 20% commission only upon conversion.

What about privacy and compliance?

We operate under NZ’s Privacy Act 2020. We connect individuals with licensed advisers; all actual financial advice remains the responsibility of the advising professional.
